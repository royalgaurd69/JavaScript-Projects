Preventive Maintenance Service

Our comprehensive preventive maintenance service is designed to keep your vehicle running smoothly and prevent costly repairs down the road. Regular maintenance is essential for extending the life of your vehicle and ensuring optimal performance.

What's Included:

  • Oil and filter change
  • Tire rotation and pressure check
  • Brake inspection and adjustment
  • Fluid levels check and top-up
  • Battery test and terminal cleaning
  • Air filter replacement
  • Spark plug inspection
  • Belt and hose inspection
  • Suspension and steering check
  • Comprehensive multi-point inspection

Our certified technicians use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to identify potential issues before they become major problems. We recommend preventive maintenance every 5,000 miles or 6 months, whichever comes first.

Body Repair Service

Our body repair service is designed to restore your vehicle's appearance and structural integrity after accidents or damage. We use advanced techniques and high-quality materials to ensure your vehicle looks as good as new.

What's Included:

  • Dent removal and repair
  • Panel replacement
  • Frame straightening
  • Paint matching and application
  • Scratch removal
  • Bumper repair or replacement
  • Window and windshield replacement
  • Paint protection coating
  • Rust repair and prevention
  • Post-repair quality inspection

Our skilled technicians have years of experience in body repair and use the latest tools and techniques to restore your vehicle to its pre-accident condition. We work with all insurance companies to make the repair process as smooth as possible.

Car Care Service

Our premium car care service is designed to keep your vehicle looking its best inside and out. From basic washing to complete detailing, we offer a range of car care services to suit your needs and budget.

What's Included:

  • Exterior wash and wax
  • Interior vacuum and cleaning
  • Dashboard and console detailing
  • Leather conditioning
  • Glass cleaning and polishing
  • Tire dressing
  • Engine bay cleaning
  • Headlight restoration
  • Paint protection application
  • Odor elimination

Our car care specialists use only high-quality products that are safe for your vehicle and the environment. Regular car care not only enhances the appearance of your vehicle but also helps protect it from environmental damage and preserves its value.
